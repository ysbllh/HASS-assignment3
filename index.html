2017-2023 Selected Resale flat price data, extracted from Data.gov.sg

Design, Build and Sell Scheme (DBSS) flats were introduced by HDB in 2005. DBSS projects are developed by private developers, and thus have more unique features than regular HDBs.
There are a total of 13 DBSS projects to date, before the scheme was discontinued in 2012.
DBSS flats can range from 3-room to 5-room.
